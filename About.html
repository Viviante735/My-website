About Me

I started my journey in product management after exploring various fields and discovering a passion for creating meaningful solutions. My background in early childhood education has shaped my empathy and people-first mindset, which I carry into every product decision.

Throughout my career, I’ve worked on cross-functional teams, led product discovery initiatives, created user personas, and written PRDs to guide development teams. I also have experience with customer service, which sharpens my understanding of user needs.

Outside of work, I’m always learning—whether through tech communities, reading, or mentoring others who want to break into tech.
